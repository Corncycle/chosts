WHAT IS THIS? WHAT IS NETHACK?
Nethack is a roguelike dungeon crawler released in 1987. To make a long story short, in Nethack you must obtain the fabled Amulet of Yendor and sacrifice it to your god on the Astral Plane. In this browser version*, you control your player character (pictured above as the @ symbol) by clicking adjacent tiles.
*full functionality not guaranteed
